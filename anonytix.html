Project Overview

Anonytix is a platform that allows students to provide anonymous feedback on their education experience. With features that ensure confidentiality, the platform encourages constructive feedback that can improve teaching methods and student engagement.

Problem Statement

Students are often afraid to share feedback about their education for fear of retaliation or judgment. As a result, valuable insights about teaching practices, course materials, and student satisfaction go unreported.

Solution

Anonytix provides a secure, anonymous platform for students to share feedback and concerns, ensuring confidentiality and encouraging honest communication. It also features a data analytics dashboard for principals, which aggregates feedback trends, monitors sentiment, and prioritizes issues. This allows administrators make informed decisions to improve the school environment based on real-time, actionable data.

Design Process

Empathize

Conducted focus group discussions with students to gather insights into their fears and concerns regarding feedback.

Define

Defined the need for anonymity and simplicity in feedback forms.

Ideate

Explored the use of gamification to increase student participation while maintaining confidentiality.

Prototype

Designed a simple and clean user interface for submitting feedback and viewing reports.

Test

Tested feedback forms for usability and ease of access.

Expected Impact

Increase student engagement with feedback systems by 60%.

Provide valuable insights to instructors, leading to improved teaching methods.

Foster a culture of open communication in educational institutions.
